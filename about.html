About

Our company, RainyDays, was founded by a family of outdoor enthusiasts who wanted to create high-quality clothing that could withstand the rigors of nature. We began in a small garage, creating pieces for ourselves and our friends, but as word of our durable, comfortable gear spread, we knew we had something special.


We put our hearts and souls into every piece we create, sourcing only the best materials and constantly testing our designs in the field. And because we're a small, family-owned business, we're able to remain nimble and responsive to the needs of our customers.


We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the beauty and wonder of nature, and we're committed to making that a reality by creating gear that is not only functional, but stylish too. Whether you're summiting a mountain or just enjoying a day hike, we want you to feel confident and comfortable in our clothing.


Our gear is for those who are not afraid to push boundaries, who are always looking for the next adventure, and who appreciate style as much as function. Join us on this journey and experience the difference with our outdoor clothing.
